Avocado Salad

Description

Bold, colourful, and flavourful salad featuring a delicious combination of ripe avocados, sweet onions, fresh tomatoes, and cilantro

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados, diced
  • Onion, chopped
  • Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 large Tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 Lime, juiced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Gently toss until evenly coated
